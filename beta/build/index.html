Rules

Overlay

  1. Hover On
    • A semi-transparent dark radial gradient smoothly fades into view, along with an "Open" sign
    • "Share" button and "More Options" icons slide up from the bottom right of the image, "Share Button" sliding up 0.5 seconds later
    • Cursor changes to pointer
  2. Hover Off
    • Overlay and "Open" fade out slowly
    • Icons slide down from the bottom right of the image, "Share" button sliding down 0.5 seonds later
    • Pointer returns to default cursor
  3. Click
    • Opens the specific pin page on a separate window

Share

  1. Hover On
    • Button scales up a quarter of its original size
    • Button background colour changes from a light gray colour to a white colo
  2. Hover Off
    • Button transforms back into its original, smaller shape
    • Button background returns back to its light gray colour
  3. Click
    • Button scales down to 0.75 of its original size
    • After the click, button shape changes into its original shape, revealing share options as a menu
